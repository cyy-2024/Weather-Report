Welcome to My Android App

Welcome to Weather Forecast App, an elegant and user-friendly Android app that provides accurate weather forecasts with additional features such as a music player and schedule management. This app is designed to enhance your daily life experience, not only allowing you to keep up to date with weather conditions, but also allowing you to enjoy music and effectively manage your schedule.

Features

Music Player

The music player allows you to play, pause, skip tracks, and go back to the previous song. It provides an easy-to-use interface for managing your music collection.

Schedule Manager

With the schedule manager, you can create, edit, and delete reminders for important events. Stay organized by keeping track of your daily tasks and appointments.

Weather Forecast

The weather forecast feature provides real-time weather updates based on your location. Use your own API key to get weather data for your specific region.

Download and Install

To install the app on your Android device:

  1. Download the APK file from the link below.
  2. On your Android device, go to Settings > Security and enable "Unknown Sources" to allow installation of apps from sources other than the Play Store.
  3. Open the downloaded APK file to install the app.
